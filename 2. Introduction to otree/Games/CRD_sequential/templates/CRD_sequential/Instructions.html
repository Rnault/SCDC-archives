Climate Game: instructions

In this study, you will join a random group of {{ Constants.players_per_group }} players. Each participant in the group is given {{ Constants.endowment }} at the beginning of the game. However, a huge catastrophe is coming and none of that money will be useful if the world ends. But the group has the opportunity to avoid that disaster. To do so, they have to contribute a minimum of {{ Constants.target }} in total over 10 rounds. Each round, each participant in the group decides how much she/he is going to contribute to save the planet. Contribution could be any integer from 0 to {{ Constants.endowment }}.

By the end of the game, you simply keep the money you have left. If the goal was reached, you're left alive to happily enjoy another day with your money. Otherwise, if the group does not reach the target contribution of {{ Constants.target }}, everyone has {{ Constants.losing }}% chance of losing everything. If the goal of {{ Constants.target }} has been reached before the last round starts, you will still need to go through all the remaining rounds to finish the game.

Press the "next" button whenever you are ready to start playing.
